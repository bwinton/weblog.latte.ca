Gold ring, halfling,<br>
Man gone, but for song,<br>
Wizard fell, into hell,<br>
Mirror of elf, see the south,<br>
Forest bent, met an Ent,<br>
Isengard strong, soon to be gone.<br>
<br>
Into the black land, they will stand.<br>
<br>
Gold ring, halfling.<br>
